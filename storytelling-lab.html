Storytelling Lab

Tucked inside our bookshop, the Storytelling Lab offers programming for kids after school, on weekends, and over the summer. Whether your child is a grapho-maniac always scribbling away or a somewhat reluctant writer, someone who loves to draw or someone simply learning to hold a pen, our labs foster a love of words, pictures, and stories that children will carry with them for the rest of their lives.

Our Labs: Workshops, Drop-ins and Master Classes

Our Storytelling Lab is a home for storytellers of all ages, where we hold classes ranging from workshops on the art of the short story to bookmaking classes for younger writers and drop-in art and storytelling sessions. All taught and facilitated by real life working writers.

Tell us the kinds of stories you most want to read and write!

Labs

This fall in the Storytelling Lab we are so excited to be offering our first full season of programming. From poetry to animation, collage to acting, your kids can explore a rich range of storytelling arts and form a community of artists and writers under the guidance of an expert teaching artist. Master Classes

Taught by amazing authors and illustrators in the Brooklyn community and beyond, our Stories Master Classes introduce young writers and artists to the world of the professional writer and offer a chance to learn directly from expert practitioners of the art form.

Our Master Classes are usually 1.5-hour sessions held on a weekday evening or weekend, and focus on some element of storytelling of the master author or artist's choosing.

Storytimes

We host Storytimes in our Storytelling Lab every Sunday morning at 10:30 AM, usually hosting an author or illustrator. See our Bookshop page for our upcoming events. We also hold Storytimes with our staff every Friday morning at 11 AM.

All of our Storytimes are free and first come, first served basis. They can fill up, so come early to guarantee a seat!

Additional Programming

Birthday Parties

We host storytelling Birthday Parties, which can be customized to explore the kinds of stories and activities your child loves the most. Parties are generally two hours long and can include story-related games, writing activities, crafts, and, of course, cake or cupcakes!

Birthday parties can accommodate up to 12 children. For more information, e-mail us at parties@storiesbk.com

Camps — Coming in 2017

Our Stories School break and Summer Camps will be filled with games, exercises and crafts that will culminate in a journal campers design and create together. These will expand on our Stories Workshops. Kids will experiment and play with words and stories in a range of games and activities, read good stories, have focused time for creating, comment on the work of their friends, and have a chance to revise and polish a final piece, which will be published in a journal of the Stories Summer Writers. We will also provide a snack and spend some time outside.
